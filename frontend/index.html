Our Story

How we came about

It started as a dream: To create a corner of Paris in the heart of Stockholm. A place where authentic falvours, freshly baked pastries and genuine care is our main focus.

Everything we bake is made by hand and fresh each morning. From our buttery croissants to our delicate macarons. To complement these flavours, our coffee is brewed with the same care using beans from selected roasters.

At our Cafe, you are welcome to take a break from everyday life and relax or quickly order online and pick up on the go.
